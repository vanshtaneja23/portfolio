Machine Learning

Developed and deployed an SMS Spam Classifier using an ensemble soft-voting approach that combined Naive Bayes, Support Vector Classifier (SVC), and Extra Trees to improve robustness and predictive accuracy. Implemented a comprehensive preprocessing pipeline, including lowercasing, tokenization, stemming/lemmatization, stopword and punctuation removal, and TF-IDF vectorization, to effectively capture semantic patterns within short-text messages. Designed techniques to reduce false positives on benign short messages by introducing a HAM booster for greetings and common safe phrases. Conducted extensive model evaluation with accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and confusion matrices to ensure a balanced trade-off between spam detection and minimizing ham misclassification. Achieved perfect test performance (100% precision and recall) across a dataset of 60,000 labeled SMS messages.
